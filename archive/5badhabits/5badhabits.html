A FLATMATE'S CONFESSIONS

Always trying to think of what I'd like to write in a blog post has led to some serious self-reflection. I think it's about time I confessed a few things to you, flat-mates. I know some of these atrocities have already come to light, but others certainly haven't. And I can sense your suspicion...

So, Prepare your butts: here comes 5 bad habits I picked up whilst living with other people

Confession Number 1 goes to you, Danny. Yeah I think you already knew. I mean, we both know you didn't eat it that quickly.

DANNY
FLUFF
I bought you some of that delicious American marshmallow stuff in a big tub for your birthday. And, on several ocassions, I snuck into your cupboard, pulled it from the back and straight up ate that stuff. I know how you are about other people's germs so I made sure to use my hands to scoop it out.

Danny, I'm sorry.

Number 2. You were never going to make a cake. It's as simple as that. You said you would, but we all knew you wouldn't.

SAM
CHOCOLATE ICING
This, at the time, infamous, sin was one of the most enjoyable. Mostly, though not exclusively, at night I would climb up to your cupboard, Sam, and dig in to that delicious chocolate cake icing. With Pav's help, the whole tub was polished off within about a year. We would spoon it into our mouths and sometimes onto toast.

Sam, I'm sorry

James, you're in at number 3. I know I left you a note that one time apologising, but, in the words of Usher: "There's more. Listen."

JAMES
HONEY
After that first time where I opened the seal on the bottle, it's only been downhill. The squeezy bottle honey just gets squeezed straight down the gullet now. It's a no-clean-up job, you see. This has only been a recent development and I don't mean to take it as far as the others so I'm confessing now in an effort to curtail further trespasses.

James, I'm sorry.

Number 4 is you, Alex! I bet you're ripping your dick off with frustration at this very moment. How dare I! I know, I know. Please, let me continue...

ALEX
TOILET ROLL
Yes! Your toilet roll! When you were away in Surrey for most of the summer of 2012 I would actually go into your room and pick from your private stash (and what a stash it was) of expensive toilet roll. I didn't buy any myself all summer.This is actually a double confession because, Sam, when Alex asked me about it, I said it was you.

Alex (and Sam), I'm sorry.

Number 5 is remarkably similar to number 2.

SAM
CHOCOLATE ICING (AGAIN)
So, after I wrote you that letter and left it inside the empty tub of chocolate icing, you found it and so I actually bought you a new tub of chocolate icing. But I ate that one as well. If nothing else I'm consistent.

I'm actually sorry, Sam.
