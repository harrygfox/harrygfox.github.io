FUNKY AARDVARK

I decided to take on a live brief and rebrand the Funky Aardvark, an art organisation based in Chester. I had a lot of fun researching and getting to know the people involved in the "Aardvark community".

This is my final rationale explaining the identity concept I created, a mock-up shop window maquette, chalk logo, and branded stationary.

CHESHIRE ASPERGERS PARENT SUPPORT

The concept I cam up with for the ChAPS charity is based around a butterfly, as the connotations of transformation, change, individuality, and being a social butterfly fit well with all the ideas that the ChAPS group wish to promote

The logo is built upon two eternity rings to symbolise the constant support ChAPS offer their clients.

JONATHAN PRICE LANDSCAPES

A Manchester-based landscape gardener got in touch and was looking for a new brand identity. My Price is a retired musician and this comes across in his garden designs.

I used his musical influences to create a simple identity that reflects his personality, as a musician and as a landscape gardener.
